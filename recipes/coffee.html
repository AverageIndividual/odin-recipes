How to make a cup of coffee

Description

Needing a pick me up in the morning is a problem a lot of people have. Coffee is one of the worlds most popular drinks and to a non coffee drinking it can be intimidating. Fear not dear reader I have you covered. In this section I will show you how to make coffee using a coffee maker.

Ingredients

  • Coffee of your choice
  • Coffee creamer of your choice
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Start by adding water to your coffee maker
  2. Add 3 scoops of coffee to the top with a coffee filter
  3. Start the coffee maker
  4. Wait for the the coffee to finish and grab your favorite coffee mug!
  5. Pour finished coffee into the mug and add a much (or as little) of sugar and creamer as you desire
  6. Enjoy! Caution: It will be hot!
