How to make Scrambled Eggs for Non-Cooks (Like me)

Description

Today we are entering the world of poultry. There are many different ways to make eggs but this is my personal favorite. Following this recipe will end in a beautiful, fluffy, end product that is not only aesthetically pleasing but also tastes amazing!

Ingredients

  • 2 unboiled eggs
  • Pinch of salt
  • Pinch of pepper
  • 1 Tablespoon of butter

Steps

  1. Grab a bowl and a spoon
  2. Break the two eggs into the bowl and mix until eggs turn completely yellow
  3. Start by placing a pan on the stove
  4. Put the butter in the pan and turn the stove top on medium-high heat
  5. When the butter is melted, add the bowl of eggs to the pan
  6. Using a spatula, mix the eggs while cooking to scramble
  7. Once the eggs are starting to harden add the salt and pepper
  8. Once the egg has completely turned to a solid, turn stove off and enjoy!
